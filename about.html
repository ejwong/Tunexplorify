Playlist Modifier


Problem:

Currently, Spotify has a lot of playlists that allow users to discover new music through browsing playlists and updating Discover Weekly, a specifically generated playlist aligned with a user's music tastes. Also, people share music through playlists with their friends. However, users may not like every song in the playilst, but still want to conveniently keep track of the songs that they do enjoy.


Solution:

Playlist Modifier allows users to easily preview songs in a playlist that they encounter and rate them with a "like", "maybe", or "dislike". A new playlist will be created within their Spotify library with the tracks that they like at the top and the tracks that they might like at the bottom for them to come back and listen to in the future. This reduces the hassle of having to listen to a playlist with songs that they dislike.
